BALANCING

HIGH-SPEED TIRE BALANCING & FLAT REPAIR

Unbalanced tires cause vibrations, uneven wear, and a rough ride. We use high-speed computerized balancing equipment to eliminate these issues, extending the life of your tires and giving you a smoother, quieter drive.

Got a flat tire? We're open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We handle puncture repair, patching, and plugging quickly and affordably. If a tire can't be repaired safely, we'll find you the most cost-effective replacement from our inventory.

SHOCKS & STRUTS

SHOCKS & SUSPENSION REPAIR

Worn shocks and struts make your ride bouncy, hurt your handling, and increase braking distance. If your car feels loose over bumps, dips excessively when braking, or your tires are wearing unevenly, your suspension probably needs attention.

We inspect and replace shocks, struts, control arms, ball joints, tie rods, and sway bar links on all makes and models, both foreign and domestic. Paterson roads are rough, and good suspension keeps you safe and comfortable.

We'll give you an honest assessment and only replace what actually needs replacing. No guesswork, no unnecessary parts.

ALIGNMENT

PRECISION WHEEL ALIGNMENT

Car pulling to one side? Steering wheel off-center? Tires wearing unevenly? You need a wheel alignment. We use modern alignment equipment to correct these issues, improving handling, extending tire life, and boosting fuel efficiency.

Paterson roads are brutal on alignment. One bad pothole can knock your wheels out of spec. We recommend checking your alignment every 6,000 miles or anytime you hit a big pothole, curb, or notice uneven tire wear.

Quick turnaround, honest pricing, and we'll show you the before and after readings so you can see exactly what we corrected.
