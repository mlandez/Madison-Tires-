OUR STORY

BUILT ON TRUST, DRIVEN BY SERVICE

Madison Avenue Tires & Wheels started with a simple idea: give Paterson drivers honest tire service at honest prices. Located right on Madison Avenue, we've served thousands of customers across Passaic County, from flat tire emergencies at 3 AM to full brake overhauls and wheel alignments.

Our team treats every car like it's our own. Whether you need affordable new tires, used tires, tire balancing, or a complete brake repair, we take the time to explain what's going on and never upsell what you don't need. That's why drivers from Paterson, Clifton, Passaic, Totowa, and Wayne keep coming back.
