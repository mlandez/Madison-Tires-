TIRE BALANCING

PRECISION BALANCING & 24-HOUR FLAT REPAIR

Unbalanced tires cause vibrations, uneven wear, and a rough ride. At Madison Tires & Wheels, we use precision computerized balancing equipment to eliminate these issues, extending the life of your tires and giving you a smoother, quieter drive.

Got a flat tire? Because we're open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, we can help you anytime — even at 2 AM on a holiday. We handle puncture repair, patching, and plugging quickly and affordably.
